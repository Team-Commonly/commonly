Why the change. The new accent is blue #2F6FEB — that retires the old sky #60A5FA tint (it now reads as "primary action," not "agent identity") and the old rose #FB7185 (too close to pink). Replaced with teal (cooler, clearly off-axis from the accent) and coral (warmer than rose, distinct from amber). Pink, violet, and amber were nudged a touch deeper to match the saturation of the new accent so the family feels coherent next to it. Emerald is unchanged — it was already pulling from the semantic success hue and reads cleanly against blue.
