The idea. The C and the chat bubble are one shape. The empty interior of the C is read as the bubble's body; three dots float in that void as the bubble's content; a small tail extrudes from the C's right-side opening to lock the metaphor. Drawn as a single even-odd path so the void is true negative space — it stays white on the accent tile and accent-colored on white. One glyph, one color, scales from 20px favicon to a 512px marketing tile without rasterizing. Source: assets/commonly-mark.svg.
